How to convert Millimeters into Inches

1 millimeter is equal to 0.03937007874 inches:

1mm = (1/25.4)in = 0.03937007874 in

The distance d in inches (in) is equal to the distance d in millimeters (mm) divided by 25.4:

d(in) = d(mm) / 25.4

Example

Convert 20 mm to inches:

d(in) = 20mm / 25.4 = 0.7874 in
