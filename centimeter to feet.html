How to convert Meters into Inches

1 centimeter is equal to 0.0328084 feet:

1 cm = (1/30.48) ft = 0.0328084 ft

The distance d in feet (ft) is equal to the distance d in centimeters (cm) divided by 30.48:

d(ft) = d(cm) / 30.48

Example

Convert 20 centimeters to feet:

d(ft) = 20cm / 30.48 = 0.65617ft
