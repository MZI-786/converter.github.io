How to convert Feet into Inches

1 foot is equal to 12 inches:

1ft = 12in

The distance d in inches (in) is equal to the distance d in feet (ft) times 12:

d(in) = d(ft) x12

Example

Convert 2 feet to inches:

d(in) = 2ft x 12 = 24in
