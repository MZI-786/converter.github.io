How to convert Meters into Inches

1 foot is equal to 30.48 centimeters:

1 ft = 30.48 cm

The distance d in centimeters (cm) is equal to the distance d in feet (ft) times 30.48:

d(cm) = d(ft) × 30.48

Example

Convert 2 feet to centimeters:

d(cm) = 2ft × 30.48 = 60.96cm
