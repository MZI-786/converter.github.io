How to convert centimeters to inches

1 centimeter is equal to 0.3937007874 inches:

1cm = (1/2.54)in = 0.3937007874in

The distance d in inches (in) is equal to the distance d in centimeters (cm) divided by 2.54:

d(in) = d(cm) / 2.54
