How to convert Inches to Meters

1 inch is equal to 1/12 feet:

1in = 1/12ft = 0.083333ft

The distance d in feet (ft) is equal to the distance d in inches (in) divided by 12:

d(ft) = d(in) / 12

Example

Convert 20 inches to feet:

d(ft) = 20in / 12 = 1.667ft
