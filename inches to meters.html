How to convert Inches to Meters

1 inch is equal to 0.0254 meters:

1 inch = 0.0254m

The distance d in meters (m) is equl to the distance d in inches (in) times 0.0254:

d(m)=d(in)x 0.0254

Example

Convert 20 inches to meters:

d(m)= 20in x 0.0254 = 0.508m
