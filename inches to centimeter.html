How to convert inches to centimeters

1 inch is equal to 2.54 centimeters:

1in = 2.54cm

The distance d in centimeters (cm) is equal to the distance d in inches (in) times 2.54:

d(cm) = d(inch) × 2.54

Example

Convert 20 inches to centimeters:

d(cm) = 20in × 2.54 = 50.8cm
