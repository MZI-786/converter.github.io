How to convert Centimeters into Millimeters

1 centimeter is equal to 10 millimeters:

1 cm = 10 mm

The distance d in millimeters (mm) is equal to the distance d in centimeters (cm) times 10:

d(mm) = d(cm)x10

Example

Convert 20 cm to millimeters:

d(mm) = 20cm x 10 = 200mm
