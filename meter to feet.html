How to convert Meters into Feet

1 meter is equal to 0.3.28084 feet:

1 m = (1/0.3048) ft = 3.28084 ft

The distance d in feet (ft) is equal to the distance d in meters (m) divided by 0.3048:

d(ft) = d(m) / 0.3048

Example

Convert 2 meters to feet:

d(ft) = 2m / 0.3048 = 6.5617ft
