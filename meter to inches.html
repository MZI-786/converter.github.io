How to convert Meters into Inches

1 meter is equal to 39.37007874 inches:

1 m = (1/0.0254)" = 39.37007874in

The distance d in inches (in) is equl to the distance d in meters (m) divided by 0.0254:

d(in)=d(m)/0.0254

Example

Convert 0.2 meters to inches:

d(in)= 0.2m / 0.0254 = 7.874in
