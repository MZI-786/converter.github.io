How to convert Inches to Millimeters

1 inch is equal to 25.4 millimeters:

1 in = 25.4mm

The distance d in millimeters (mm) is equal to the distance d in inches (in) times 25.4:

d(mm) = d(in) x 25.4

Example

Convert 20 inches to millimeters:

d(mm) = 20in x 25.4 = 508mm
