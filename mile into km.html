How to convert Miles into kilometers

1 Mile is equal to 1.609344 Kilometers:

1 mi = 1.609344 km

The distance d in kilometers (km) is equl to the distance d in miles (mi) times 1.609344:

d(km)=d(mi)x 1.609344

Example

Convert 20 miles to kilometers:

d(km)= 20mi x 1.609344 = 32.18688km
